What we do with your money

Baxia uses a variety of methods to ensure the safety and security of retail client funds.

Overview

Client funds are independently deposited in regulated bank Client accounts

Client funds and assets are stored separately and completely separated from Baxia's own funds

Authorized and supervised by the Financial Services Authority of Seychelles (FSA) and Securities Commission of the Bahamas (SCB)

Your money and assets are never merged with Baxia’s own money or assets

Baxia doesn’t use your money for business activities, such as paying staff or rent

What does Baxia do with your money?

Investment firms like Baxia differ from ordinary banks. Mostly because investment firms similar to Baxia are required to separate client money and assets from their own resources. That means we are not allowed to use client money and assets in the course of our business activities, and clients funds are completely separated and protected in the unlikely event that Baxia becomes insolvent.

Baxia Markets is authorized by the Financial Services Authority of Seychelles (FSA) and Securities Commission of the Bahamas (SCB) to operate an investment business. FSA and SCB require firms they oversee to keep clients funds segregated from their own.

What happens to my funds after they are deposited into my Baxia account?

Since your funds are held in segregated bank accounts, this means that the funds remain yours, rather than Baxia’s. This also ensures that it’s readily identifiable as client money, so Baxia and its creditors will not have any charge, liens or rights of set-off and retention rights.

We have segregated bank accounts with several trusted top international banks such as ABSA.

Encryption Technique

Baxia Markets enforces the SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) network security protocol to guarantee secure transactions with our clients, protect customer information during communication exchanges, and keep customer details confidential.

  • User identification and server authentication policies ensure that data is transmitted only to the authorized parties.
  • All data transmission is encrypted to prevent unauthorized third parties from accessing or stealing the data.
  • Ensure that data integrity is maintained and no changes occur during transmission.
