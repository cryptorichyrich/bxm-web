    Learn More About Trading Objectives Below:

    Profit Share Up to

    Funded Prop BX offers a 15% profit share from the profits generated during the challenge steps (Both Classic and Express Challenges). We want traders to be rewarded for their discipline and knowledge during their entire journey.

    For a $6000 Classic Challenge, this means that if you reach your profit target of $6000 in Step 1, your profit target of $300 in Step 2 and become a funded trader, you are eligible to receive $90 (15% of $600) plus $45 (15% of $300), for a total of $135.

    Back to top of table

    Profit Target

    Funded Prop BX enables traders to qualify for a funded account by meeting modest profit targets. Upon reaching these targets, traders can commence trading with their funded account.

    In the Classic Challenge phases, traders are expected to secure a 10% profit target in their Step 1 and a 5% profit target in their Step 2 accounts, respectively.

    For a $6000 Classic Challenge, this means that you are expected to reach a profit target of $600 in Step 1, and a profit target of $300 in Step 2. Similarly, the profit target for a $6000 Express Challenge is 25% of the initial balance, which is $1500.

    Back to top of table

    Maximum Daily Loss

    Both the Classic and the Express Challenges set a daily loss limit at 5% of your initial account balance. This means that your daily net loss, including both open and closed positions, can not exceed this threshold.

    For a $6000 Classic Challenge account, your maximum daily loss is capped at $300. Similarly, for a $6000 Express challenge account, your maximum daily loss is capped at 5% of your initial balance or $300.

    Overstepping this limit constitutes a rule violation, and your daily loss limit resets at midnight server time.

    Back to top of table

    Maximum Overall Loss

    The maximum allowable loss is limited to 10% of the account balance in both the Classic Challenge steps as well as the single Express Challenge step.

    For a $6000 Classic Challenge, this means your account balance/equity must not drop below 90% (or $5400) of the initial balance at any point during the Classic Challenge in either Step 1 or Step 2.

    Similarly, for a $6000 Express Challenge account, your account balance/equity must not drop below 90% (or $5400) of the initial balance at any point during the single step challenge.

    Back to top of table

    Trading Period

    The Classic Challenge includes a maximum time frame of 4 weeks for achieving the profit target in step 1 and a maximum time frame of 8 weeks for achieving the profit target in step 2. However, there are no time limits for the single step Express Challenge.

    Back to top of table

    Minimum Trading Days

    Traders are required to execute at least 5 distinct trades over 5 different days in the Classic Challenge. Traders are required to execute at least 10 distinct trades over 10 different days in the Express Challenge.

    Back to top of table

    Commissions

    Funded Prop BX offers traders commissions as low as $3 per lot on FX pairs and no commissions on Metals, Indices, Crypto and Energy.

    Back to top of table

    Profit Split Upto (BX Funded Account)

    Traders can get up to 90% profit split in the Funded Prop BX Account with a scaling plan.

    Back to top of table

    Leverage

    Funded Prop BX offers a professional leverage up to 1:100 on FX pairs and 1:50 on Indices, Metals and Energy.

    Back to top of table

    News Trading

    To offer flexibility to our traders, we allow news trading in the Classic Challenge accounts. However, news trading option is not available in the single step Express Challenge.

    Back to top of table

    Weekend Holding

    While holding a trade over the weekend is risky, Funded Prop BX allows traders to hold trades open during the weekend in the Classic and the Express Challenges.

    Back to top of table

    Expert Advisors

    Funded Prop BX allows use of Expert Advisors in both the Classic and Express Challenges to assist traders.

    Back to top of table

    Trade Copiers

    Use of Trader Copiers is supported in the Classic and Express Challenge to assist traders.

    Back to top of table

    Reset Option Discount

    In case of a rule violation leading to account suspension, traders can rejoin the program at a discounted reset fee. This allows a fresh start in your trading journey with a 10% discount for the Classic Challenge Step 1 or a 20% discount for the Express Challenge Step 1.

    Back to top of table

    Refundable Fee

    Once you are promoted to a Funded Prop BX Account, your registration fee is refundable, further supporting your trading career progression.
