Spreads

 

Baxia Markets offers tight variable spreads between the bid and ask price in our MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 forex trading platforms. We are able to offer industry leading spreads in our platforms thanks to our pool of liquidity providers with True ECN connectivity. Our pricing consists of over 20 different superior banks and dark pool liquidity sources ensuring our spreads remain tight and liquidity deep 24/5 beating many competing forex brokers. 

 

The advantage our clients have is that they enter the forex arena on the same conditions as the largest financial institutions. Prices are streamed from various liquidity providers to Baxia’s Aggregation Engine which then selects the best BID and ASK prices from the streamed prices to be quoted to our clients. During liquid times, our spreads can go as low as 0 pips on our BxZero account. Our minimum and average spreads are listed below:

  • "Spread" in the forex market can be described as the difference between the buying and selling prices of a quote for any particular pair. For example, let's say we had a EUR/USD bid (sell) price of 1.10020 and ask (buy) price of 1.10032, the spread would be 1.2pips.
