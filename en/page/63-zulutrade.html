What is ZuluTrade?

 

ZuluTrade is the world's biggest copying trading platform that allows users to choose among 100,000  forex and CFDs professional traders from all over the world, to receive trades in real time in their own Baxia trading account.

 

To get started, you just need to open a live account with us and link it to Zulutrade. Once your account is linked, you will be able to select traders according to an efficient algorithm ranking and/or per your preferred strategy. Once you start following their trades, they will be reflected directly into your broker account. 

 

Special ZuluTrade Features Include: 

 

  • Traders’ Combos - Diversify your investments by choosing multiple forex strategy providers in a way to maximize your returns simply and efficaciously.

  • Portfolio Simulator - Simulate the forex strategies before start trading, get to know your potential profit and losses according to statistics based on historical data.
