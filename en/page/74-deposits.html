Getting started

Funding Currency

 

Baxia Markets enables clients to open trading accounts and deposit funds in the following base currencies:

 

  • United States Dollar (USD)
  • Australian Dollar (AUD) 
  • Euro (EUR) 
  • Pound Sterling (GBP) 

 

We also provide several payment methods from the most traditional as credit and debit card to local deposits methods available for LATAM clients.

Funding Methods:

 

Credit / Debit Card Deposits

(Baxia FX accepts VISA and MasterCard)

 

  • Minimum deposit: $50 USD
  • Fee: Free
  • Time to clear into trading account: 30-60 min (in business hours)

 

Neteller

 

  • Minimum deposit: $50 USD
  • Fee: Free
  • Time to clear into trading account: 30-60 min (in business hours)

 

Skrill

 

  • Minimum deposit: $50 USD
  • Fee: Free
  • Time to clear into trading account: 30-60 min (in business hours)

 

International Bank Wire Transfer

 

  • Minimum deposit: $50 USD
  • Fee: Free
  • Time to clear into trading account: 2-5 business days

 

Local Methods

 

  • Minimum deposit: $50 USD
  • Fee: Varies (check your Client Area)
  • Time to clear into trading account: Varies (check your Client Area)
