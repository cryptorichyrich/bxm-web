Technology Execution

Baxia operates as a Straight Through Process Broker (STP). A STP broker does not have a dealing desk, instead our system sends the order to the liquidity providers and they fill the client’s order at the best available price, which ensures that you get the best possible rate from our LP’s pool at the moment you place your order

What Makes Baxia Different?

For the first time, traders will be able to choose the location of their servers in addition to choosing their account types. This gives traders trading with Baxia Markets the unique edge of choosing their liquidity pools. 

 

  • Baxia London infrastructure gives traders low latency access to European financial institutions. 
  • Baxia New York infrastructure gives traders low latency access to US financial institutions. 
  • Choice of either BxStandard or BxZero spreads with Baxia London and Baxia New York.
  • Traders can allocate their strategies across multiple pools of liquidity.
