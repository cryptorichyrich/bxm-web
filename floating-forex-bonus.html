    For a limited time only, Baxia Markets is offering a huge 40% Floating Deposit Bonus to all traders.

    In simple terms, a floating bonus supports trading drawdown and allows the trader an opportunity to recover their position before it is margin called or stopped out. The floating bonus is a unique offering of Baxia Markets to allow traders to manage positions during volatile times and reducing the need for additional deposits.

    • The floating bonus can not only be used to open larger position sizes, it also allows the trader to stay in the market longer in case markets move against the trader.
    • The bonus does not have an expiration period, allowing a trader to keep their position open as long as they can maintain the minimum margin to keep the position open.
    • There are no withdrawal limits on profits generated in the Floating Bonus account. In addition, there are no minimum lot requirements for traders to keep their floating bonus. The bonus is cancelled when a withdrawal is processed.
